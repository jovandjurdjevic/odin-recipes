Meatballs

Just like your moms!

Meal every person should know how to make

We are sure you can remember that beautiful smell when you get back home from long day at school, smell of moms meatballs, and we will help you cook make them just as they were

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • tomato sauce
  • garlic
  • salt
  • pepper
  • flour

Steps

  1. First mix beef with salt, garlic and pepper
  2. add flour to beef and make it round shaped
  3. put some salt in already boiling tomato sauce
  4. and put meatballs in tomato sauce
  5. cook 50 minutes

And voilla you have made your childhood meatballs, enjoy
