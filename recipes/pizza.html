Pizza

Mama mia!

The recipe for a true Italian pizza

Our favorite fast food made into a real lunch

Ingredients

  • flour
  • cheese
  • sausage
  • ham
  • olives

Steps

  1. mix flour with water and liitle bit of milk
  2. shape it in circle an fold the ends
  3. put cheese, sausage and ham on it
  4. put it in the oven and bake for 15 minutes
  5. when finished put olives on pizza

Tast, fast and caloric launch. Enjoy!
