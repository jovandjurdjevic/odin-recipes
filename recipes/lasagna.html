lasagna

Bon apetit!

The traditional lasagna recipe straight fom Italy by Jamie Oliver

In just 25 minutes you can bake this tasty plate

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • noodles
  • salt
  • mozarella cheese
  • tomato sauce
  • garlic
  • italian sausage
  • ground beef

Steps

  1. put eggs in bowl and mix them with flour
  2. add garlic to tomato sauce
  3. chop sausage and mix it with ground beef
  4. cook sausage and beef for 15 minutes
  5. pour sauce over sausage and beef and cook it for 10 minutes

And that is it, in just 25 minutes you have made lasagnas like Jamie Oliver
